The Philosophy

Why this exists

Most premed consulting costs between $4,000 and $10,000. That price point doesn't reflect the quality of advice — it reflects who can afford access. Talcott Ridge was built on the belief that a thoughtful, recent applicant who has navigated the current AMCAS landscape can offer more relevant guidance than a physician who applied two decades ago, at a fraction of the cost.

This is not a firm. It's a one-person advising practice run by a first-year medical student. That means limited capacity, honest turnaround times, and a genuine interest in your success — not your invoice.

🎯

Recency Advantage
