Service Detail

What each service actually covers

✍️

Personal Statement Review

Structural feedback, narrative clarity, voice, and opening hook. Not just grammar — the framing of who you are and why medicine.

📄

Activities Section Review

All 15 AMCAS entries reviewed for impact framing, keyword alignment, and honest presentation of your clinical, research, and service experiences.

🗺️

Activity Mapping

For longitudinal partners: we audit what you've done, identify gaps, and build a semester-by-semester plan to strengthen your profile before you apply.

📅

Monthly Check-Ins

One hour per month for longitudinal partners. GPA check, narrative arc review, upcoming deadlines, and whatever's keeping you up at night.

🏫

School List Consultation

Data-driven school list building based on your MCAT, GPA, state of residence, and mission fit — not just a spreadsheet of rankings.

💬

One-Off Questions

Email-based Q&A for anything from "is this experience worth listing?" to "should I address this grade on my application?" No question is too small.

What I don't do: I don't write your essays for you, I don't guarantee outcomes, and I don't pretend that any single service is the difference between acceptance and rejection. Admissions is holistic and committee-driven. What I can offer is an honest, informed second set of eyes.
